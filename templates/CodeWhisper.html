The objective of the website is to help students in Computer Science better understand the lectures and get better learning outcomes.

NotesHelper

The system generates concise notes for computer courses through ASR—speech recognition, OCR—image recognition, and text extraction processing.

Additional Sources

Based on the recognized CS terms and the student's learning content, the system can recommend subsequent learning paths, such as GitHub links or research papers.

Voice Teaching Assistant

When students encounter complex terms or concepts during learning, the system can automatically provide additional explanations or examples based on ASR.
